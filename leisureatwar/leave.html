American Soldiers on Leave

When American G.I.s were stationed overseas, they often found themselves with extra free time in the evening or on weekends. With a pass to leave their post, soldiers were free to explore their locale, at least as far as their feet could carry them. In the nearest town, they might find a cinema showing the latest Abbott and Costello, or stop by the Red Cross for dinner.
